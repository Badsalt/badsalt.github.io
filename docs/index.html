DEGERMAN LAB

-Epigenetics and telomere biology

Sofie Degerman’s research team holds expertsise in the fields of epigenetics, telomere biology, and hematopathology, and aims to improve diagnostics, risk stratification, and treatment of hematological malignancies with a special focus on T-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia (T-ALL). The research is carried out in the Translational Research Centre at Umeå University, which is an excellent translational research environment equipped with modern molecular biology technologies.
